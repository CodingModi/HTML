DIWALI

Diwali is one of the most popular festivals in India. It is known as the Festival of Lights. Diwali is celebrated by Hindus, Jains, Sikhs, and many other people. Before Diwali, people clean and decorate their homes. On this day, people wear new clothes, light lamps (diyas), and decorate their houses with rangoli. Families worship Goddess Lakshmi and pray for happiness and prosperity. People exchange gifts and sweets with friends and relatives. Children enjoy fireworks, although many people now prefer eco-friendly celebrations to reduce pollution.
